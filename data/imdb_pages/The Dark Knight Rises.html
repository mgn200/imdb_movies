Trivia

Anne Hathaway, who plays Catwoman, had been cast as Black Cat (Felicia Hardy) in Новый Человек-паук (2012) in 2010, which at that time was under Sam Raimi's direction as "Spider-Man 4" and was going to feature the Vulture and Black Cat. See more »

Goofs

During the scenes at Wayne Manor (which was filmed at Wollaton Hall in Nottingham, UK) we see that the building is made of limestone. Yet during several scenes in the interior of the building, red bricks can be seen outside the windows, showing that the interior scenes were obviously shot at a different location or in a studio.
